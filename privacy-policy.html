Privacy Policy

How UrbanFlow Works collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: July 6, 2026

UrbanFlow Works ("we", "our", "us") operates software products including Niyaz Mocks (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Google account information (name, email and profile photo).
  • Learning progress, mock test attempts, scores and analytics.
  • Device information and diagnostics.
  • Information voluntarily submitted through support requests.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Authenticate your account.

  • Provide analytics and customer support.

3. Third-Party Services

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data is shared only with trusted service providers or where required by law.

Your information is stored using Supabase and trusted infrastructure providers. We implement reasonable safeguards, but no method of electronic storage can guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Account deletion requests are handled through our Account Deletion page.

7. Your Rights

You may access, update or request deletion of your account at any time.

8. Advertising

Some applications may display advertisements provided by trusted partners.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate consent.

10. International Processing

Your information may be processed in different countries depending on the infrastructure used by our service providers.

11. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies required for functionality, security and performance.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact page.
