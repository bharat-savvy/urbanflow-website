Account Deletion

How to permanently delete your UrbanFlow Works account and associated data.

Last updated: July 8, 2026

UrbanFlow Works respects your privacy and gives you control over your personal information. You may request permanent deletion of your UrbanFlow Works account and associated personal data for any of our applications, including Niyaz Mocks, at any time.

Account deletion is permanent. Once your request has been processed, your profile, study progress, mock test history, preferences and associated personal information cannot be restored.

How to Request Account Deletion

If account deletion is available within the application, you can submit your request directly through the app.

If you are unable to access your account or the application, you may submit your deletion request using our Contact Page.

1

Submit a Request

Contact UrbanFlow Works using the Contact Page and request permanent account deletion.

2

Verify Ownership

We may ask you to verify ownership of the account using the registered email address or other account information.

3

Processing

After verification, we will process your request and permanently delete your account and eligible personal information.

4

Completion

Most deletion requests are completed within 30 days. Once completed, the process cannot be reversed.

What Information Will Be Deleted

  • Your profile information.
  • Your email address.
  • Your profile picture.
  • Your study progress.
  • Your mock test attempts and scores.
  • Your bookmarks and preferences.
  • Analytics associated with your account.

Information That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained where required by applicable law, to prevent fraud, resolve disputes, enforce our legal agreements or comply with legal obligations. Any retained information will not be used to personally identify you unless required by law.

After Your Account Is Deleted

  • You will no longer be able to sign in using the deleted account.
  • Your learning progress will be permanently removed.
  • Your saved preferences will be deleted.
  • Your deletion request cannot be reversed.

If you have any questions regarding account deletion, please contact us through our Contact Page.
