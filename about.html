Meet Niyaz.

I started UrbanFlow Works because I was tired of seeing businesses get ripped off. Agencies were charging $5,000 upfront and $500 a month just to host a basic WordPress site.

That is unacceptable. Technology has evolved, and your software should be an asset you own, not a rent you pay.
